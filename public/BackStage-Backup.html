Track 04 · Behind the mix

Behind the Mix: How Back Stage Works

Under the hood, Back Stage is intentionally simple: just HTML, CSS, and a bit of scroll reveal. That means you can edit it with any text editor, drop in new tracks at the top, and let the browser’s built-in search do the rest.

There’s no database here, no hidden CMS. The simplicity is the feature. It keeps Zentura portable, understandable, and easy to maintain — even when the rest of the world insists everything needs a dashboard.

Future note: this is also where we’ll log the first appearance of KuroeKat as Zentura’s inaugural featured artist.

Track 01 · The story so far
Living liner notes

The Story So Far: Why Zentura Exists

Zentura started with a simple belief: creative work shouldn’t have to squeeze itself into someone else’s template just to be seen. Writers, artists, and performers deserve spaces that feel like them — not like a generic feed with their name on top.

Back Stage is one expression of that belief. It’s not polished marketing copy. It’s the running commentary of a platform being built in real time, with enough structure to be useful and enough looseness to stay alive.

Over time, these tracks will tell a story: how the studio came together, how the first artists joined, how the shows evolved, and how the whole thing stayed small enough to care while still being big enough to matter.
