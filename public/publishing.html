A different deal

You wrote it.
You own it.

The traditional publishing model takes a percentage of your sales forever. We think that's backwards. Your work is yours — before, during, and after we help you share it with the world.

Zentura makes money by being excellent at what we do, not by owning a piece of you. Our income comes from the craft of publishing — editing, design, production, distribution — not from a lifetime cut of your royalties.

Take your book anywhere. Keep every dollar of your sales. Come back because we're worth coming back to.
